### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

#### General Platform Questions

**Q: What makes this LMS different from traditional platforms?**
A: Our platform combines blockchain technology with AI to provide verifiable credentials, transparent transactions, and personalized learning experiences. Your certificates are NFTs that can be independently verified on the blockchain.

**Q: Do I need cryptocurrency experience to use this platform?**
A: No! Our platform is designed to be user-friendly for learners of all technical backgrounds. We provide guides and support for wallet setup and token transactions.

**Q: How secure is my personal information?**
A: We use enterprise-grade security measures. Your learning data is protected, and blockchain transactions ensure transparency without compromising privacy.

#### Course and Learning Questions

**Q: How do I find courses that match my interests?**
A: Use our AI recommendation engine that suggests courses based on your profile, past purchases, and learning progress. You can also browse by category or search for specific topics.

**Q: Can I access courses offline?**
A: Course materials are primarily online, but you can download certain resources for offline study. Full interactive features require an internet connection.

**Q: What happens if I don't complete a course?**
A: You retain access to purchased courses indefinitely. There's no time limit for completion, and you can resume your progress at any time.

**Q: How are assessments graded?**
A: Assessments are automatically graded through our platform. For subjective assessments, we use AI-assisted grading with human review when necessary.

#### Blockchain and Token Questions

**Q: What tokens do I need to purchase courses?**
A: Courses are priced in our native platform tokens. You can purchase tokens using popular cryptocurrencies or traditional payment methods through our integrated payment system.

**Q: How do I set up a crypto wallet?**
A: Our platform provides step-by-step guides for setting up compatible wallets. We support popular wallets like MetaMask and provide customer support for wallet integration.

**Q: What blockchain network do you use?**
A: We operate on Ethereum-compatible networks, with support for both mainnet and testnet environments for development and testing.

**Q: Can I transfer my NFT certificates to other platforms?**
A: Yes! Your NFT certificates follow standard protocols and can be viewed, transferred, or displayed on any compatible NFT marketplace or portfolio.

#### AI Chatbot Questions

**Q: What can I ask the AI chatbot?**
A: The chatbot can help with:
- Course recommendations and information
- Platform navigation and feature explanations
- Learning progress and certificate status
- Technical support for blockchain features
- General questions about course content

**Q: Is the chatbot available 24/7?**
A: Yes, our AI assistant is available around the clock to help with your questions and provide learning support.

**Q: How accurate are the chatbot's responses?**
A: Our chatbot uses advanced AI models trained on platform-specific information. While highly accurate, we recommend contacting human support for complex technical issues.

#### Payment and Billing Questions

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept:
- Platform tokens (primary method)
- Major cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.)
- Traditional payment methods through Stripe integration
- Credit/debit cards for token purchases

**Q: Are there refund policies?**
A: Digital course purchases are generally non-refundable after access is granted. However, we offer a satisfaction guarantee period for new students. Contact support for specific cases.

**Q: How do I track my transaction history?**
A: All blockchain transactions are permanently recorded and can be viewed through your account dashboard. You can also verify transactions directly on the blockchain explorer.

#### Technical Support Questions

**Q: What browsers and devices are supported?**
A: Our platform works on all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) and is mobile-responsive. We recommend using the latest browser versions for the best experience.

**Q: I'm having trouble with my wallet connection. What should I do?**
A: Try these steps:
1. Ensure your wallet extension is installed and unlocked
2. Check that you're connected to the correct network
3. Clear your browser cache and reload the page
4. Contact our support team if issues persist

**Q: How do I report a bug or technical issue?**
A: Use the feedback system in your account dashboard or contact our technical support team directly. Include details about your browser, device, and the specific issue you encountered.
